"Future Kidds Play School takes pride in its commitment to excellence, as evidenced by the prestigious accolades it has garnered over the years. In 2024, the school was honored with the Certificate of Excellence by 4 Ace Entertainment, Delhi, recognizing its outstanding contribution to early childhood education. In 2023, the school received the Big Impact Award from renowned actress Dia Mirza, in the Big Impact category, acknowledging its inspirational initiatives as part of the Inspired Pre School chain in Uttar Pradesh. Notably, the school was also recognized with the Aaj Tak Prime Time Global Leadership Award in 2018 for its exemplary leadership in the education sector. Furthermore, at the 75th Asia Education Summit in 2019, Future Kidds Play School was lauded as one of the best play schools in North India, earning the Certificate of Excellence. These accolades underscore the school's unwavering dedication to providing holistic and impactful early childhood education."
